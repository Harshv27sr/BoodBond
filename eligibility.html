Check Your Donation Eligibility

Most Critical Requirements
  • Age: 18–65 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin Level: At least 12.5 g/dL (women), 13 g/dL (men)
  • General Health: Must be healthy, free from infection or acute illness
  • No recent surgeries or major illnesses in the last 6 months
  • Not pregnant or breastfeeding
  • No blood donation within the last 3 months
Temporary Deferrals
  • Fever, cold, flu, or infection in past 7 days
  • Recent vaccination (48 hrs – 2 weeks depending on vaccine)
  • Recent travel to epidemic-prone areas
  • Minor surgery, dental work, or blood tests in past week
  • Tattoo or piercing within last 6–12 months
Permanent Deferrals
  • HIV/AIDS, Hepatitis B or C infection
  • Certain cancers (active/metastatic)
  • Serious heart, lung, or kidney disease
  • History of intravenous drug abuse
  • High-risk sexual behavior (depending on blood bank rules)
